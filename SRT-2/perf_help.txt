Atmospheric density is calculated by an empirical formula.
The formula emulates National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration data.
If you wish to see how a vehicle performs without an atmosphere, enter
a Cd value of zero.

Drag=(1/2)(Cd)(dens)(Af)(v^2)
